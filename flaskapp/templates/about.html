About Us

This website is a third party utility built to help users get more out of the Dublin bikes service.

Below are some of the services we offer:


Real-time availability data

Use the map on the home screen to visually search for bike stations in the network.

Click on a stations marker to see most recent information on available bikes and empty stands.


Real time weather data

Easily check the local weather conditions before setting out.


Individual station availability analysis

Quickly see charts of historical availability.

Predict future availabiliity.


Route Planning

Plan your trip accross dublin using the dublin bikes network.
