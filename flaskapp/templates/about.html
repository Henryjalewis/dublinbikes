About Us

This website is a third party utility built to help users get more out of the Dublin bikes network.

Below are some of the services we offer:


Interactive map with real-time availability and weather data

Use the map on the home screen to visually search for bike stations in the network.

Click on a stations marker to see most recent information on available bikes and empty stands.

Station markers are colour coded to quickly show bike availablility levels.

A real-time weather widget ensures you are aware of the conditions before setting out.

Change the map overlays for a fresh feel.


Station analytics

Quickly see charts of current and historical availability data and trends.

Predict future bike availablility, up to 5 days in advance.

Our prediction models take into account the effects of weather and trends in availability over time.


Route Planning

Plan your trip across dublin using the dublin bikes network.

Choose an origin and destination and we will ensure the stations on your route have an available bike and an empty stand respectively.

No more inconvenient surprises.

We also calculate the amount of CO2 you will save from entering the atmosphere by not driving your route, so you can feel good about yourself.
